LAZY JONES

A remake of the 1984 Commodore 64 classic

RE·64 HOTEL VISION 200

Controls

  • ←→ walk the corridors
  • ↑ enter a door · ride the lift up
  • ↓ ride the lift down
  • Space fire / action in mini-games
  • Enter start game

Your shift

You are Jones, the laziest employee the Hotel Magnificent has ever hired. Dodge the manager, the maid's trolley and the hotel ghost, then slip through every door to slack off on the arcade machines inside. Clear all 18 rooms before your three warnings run out.

Behind the doors

  • The Wall

  • Star Snatch
  • Night Driver
  • 99 Balloons
  • The Turk
  • Laser Jones
  • Forty Winks
  • The Oasis Bar

Fan-made homage to Lazy Jones (Terminal Software, 1984) by David Whittaker. All code, art and music in this remake are original.

How to play

In the hotel: walk with ← →. Step into the lift shaft on the left and press ↑ or ↓ to change floors. Stand in front of a door and press ↑ to enter.

Watch out: the manager, the maid's trolley and the ghost patrol the corridors. Touch one and you lose a warning. Three warnings and you're fired.

In the rooms: each room hides a different mini-game with a 25-second timer. Score as much as you can, then you're back in the corridor. A door closes for good once you've played it — clear all 18 to finish your shift.
